Conduent (formerly Health Solutions Plus) June 2018-present

At Conduent (formerly Health Solutions Plus), I was initialy hired as a Quality Assurance Analyst. Most of my testing was manual, but there was some API testing performed through Postman as well.

After a few months, I joined the Client Services where I still work as a Delivery Management Analyst. Our clients are healthcare insurance companies. When they believe a defect has been found, they submit a service ticket. I work with severfal of these clients and have built a rapport with them by providing swift and clear communication on investigation progress. If the reported isssue is found to not be a defect, I explain this and provide training to the client if necessary. If the issue is a defect, I attempt to find the root cause of the issue, and whether or not it is related to recent changes or a version upgrade. This information is then relayed to our development team to fix the bugs for upcoming release, and when possible, write a script in SQL to resolve the issue.

When we cannot write a script for the client, and it is an issue preventing the client from operating as expected, I do my best to attempt to come up with a workaround for the client. This helps to prevent the client from encountering this issue again, and also provides a fast and easy solution if another client reports the same issue. Communication is mainly handled through email, but occasionally I will get on a call via Microsoft Teams to gather information from the client, troubleshoot issues we cannot reproduce in local environments, or allow development to deploy changes into the client's environment remotely.

Although this role is different from what I was initially hired into, I've come to enjoy the challenges of understanding what the client is reported and how the issue occurs. I I've also become very comfortable with querying databse tables via SQL Server to investigate these issues.
